Questions about this service

Important answers before you start.

LawMart provides document preparation—not legal advice or representation.

Does LawMart decide which immigration benefit I qualify for?

No. LawMart cannot determine eligibility, select a legal strategy, or give immigration advice. Clients must identify the forms they want prepared or obtain guidance from a licensed attorney or accredited representative.

Do you communicate with USCIS for me?

LawMart does not represent clients before USCIS. We provide document preparation and administrative organization at the client’s direction.

Can you translate documents?

Translation services are not automatically included. Ask about available options and any certification requirements for your filing.

Will using a preparer guarantee approval?

No. Government agencies make all decisions, and no document preparer can guarantee an outcome.
