Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful answers before you begin

Is LawMart a law firm?

No. LawMart is a document preparation company. We do not provide legal advice, legal representation, or attorney-client services.

Can you tell me which legal option I should choose?

No. Clients make their own legal decisions. We prepare documents based on the choices and information the client provides.

Do you guarantee approval or a court outcome?

No. Courts and government agencies make their own decisions, and no document preparation company can guarantee an outcome.

What should I bring to an appointment?

Bring identification, existing court or agency paperwork, relevant dates and records, and information requested during intake.
