About LawMart

Frequently Asked Questions

What clients often ask

Is LawMart a law firm?

No. LawMart is a document preparation company. We do not provide legal advice, legal representation, or attorney-client services.

Can you tell me which option I should choose?

No. You must make your own legal decisions. We can prepare documents based on the choices and information you provide.

Do you guarantee that my filing will be approved?

No. Courts and government agencies make their own decisions, and no document preparation company can guarantee an outcome.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Bring identification, existing court or agency paperwork, relevant dates and records, and any information requested during intake.
