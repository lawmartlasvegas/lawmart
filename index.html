Is LawMart a law firm?

No. LawMart is a document preparation company. We do not provide legal advice, represent clients in court, or tell clients which legal choices to make.

What information will I need to provide?

The required information depends on the service. After the initial conversation, LawMart will provide a practical checklist tailored to the documents you request.

Can LawMart file documents for me?

Filing arrangements depend on the service and the applicable court or agency. LawMart will clearly explain the available next steps for your prepared documents.
