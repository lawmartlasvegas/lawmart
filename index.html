Is LawMart a law firm?

No. LawMart is a document preparation service. We are not attorneys, do not represent clients in court, and cannot provide legal advice.

Can you tell me which legal option I should choose?

We can provide general published information and prepare documents based on the choices you make, but only a licensed attorney can advise you about your legal rights or strategy.
