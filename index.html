Frequently asked questions

What to know before getting started.

Is LawMart a law firm?

No. LawMart is a document preparation company. We are not attorneys, do not represent clients, and do not provide legal advice.

Can you tell me which document I should use?

No. You must select the document or form you want prepared. If you are uncertain about your legal rights, strategy, or which document is appropriate, you should consult a licensed attorney.

Can you file documents for me?

Available administrative services may vary by document type and court or agency. We will explain what practical delivery or filing assistance is included before work begins.

How much does document preparation cost?

Pricing depends on the document package and complexity of the administrative work. Contact us for a clear quote before you begin.
