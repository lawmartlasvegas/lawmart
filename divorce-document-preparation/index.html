Questions about this service

Important answers before you start.

LawMart provides document preparation—not legal advice or representation.

Can LawMart tell me what I should ask for in my divorce?

No. LawMart does not give legal advice or recommend legal positions. You decide the terms and provide the instructions used to prepare the documents.

Does LawMart handle contested divorces?

LawMart prepares documents at the client’s direction. Matters involving disputes, unclear rights, or contested hearings may require advice from a licensed attorney.

Can you prepare documents when children are involved?

Document preparation may include parenting-plan, custody, and support-related forms when the client provides the required decisions and information.

How long does preparation take?

Timing depends on the complexity of the requested documents and how quickly complete information is provided. The expected timeline is discussed before work begins.
