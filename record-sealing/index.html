Questions about this service

Important answers before you start.

LawMart provides document preparation—not legal advice or representation.

Can LawMart determine whether I am eligible to seal my record?

No. Eligibility can involve legal analysis. LawMart does not give legal advice and cannot guarantee eligibility or approval.

Can every Nevada conviction be sealed?

No. Some matters may not be sealable, and waiting periods or other requirements can apply. A licensed attorney can advise you about eligibility.

Do I need my criminal-history report first?

A current criminal-history report and accurate case dispositions are commonly important for preparing a record-sealing packet.

Does LawMart guarantee the court will approve my petition?

No. The court and relevant agencies control the outcome. LawMart provides document preparation only.
