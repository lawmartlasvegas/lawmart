Questions about this service

Important answers before you start.

LawMart provides document preparation—not legal advice or representation.

Can LawMart tell me whether I need a will or a trust?

No. Choosing between estate-planning strategies is a legal decision. LawMart prepares the documents selected by the client and can refer clients to an attorney when legal advice is needed.

Can you help me choose beneficiaries or trustees?

LawMart cannot recommend whom to select. We can explain what information the forms request and prepare them using your choices.

Are notarization services included?

Availability varies. The required signing formalities and any separate notarization arrangements will be explained with the completed documents.

Can documents be updated later?

Yes, estate documents are often updated after major life changes. The appropriate document depends on the changes the client has chosen to make.
